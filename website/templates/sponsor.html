How Sponsoring the Leaderboard Works

Help reward the top bug finders on BLT by sponsoring the monthly bug bounty prize. Here's how:

  1. Enter the BCH Address: Provide the BCH address where you'd like your donation to go.
  2. Send the BCH Donation: At the end of each month, send the BCH amount directly to the top bug finder's tip address.
  3. Receive Recognition: Your contribution will be acknowledged on our leaderboard, giving you visibility within the community.

Why Sponsor the Prize?

  • Incentivize Quality: Monetary rewards encourage high-quality submissions, improving overall outcomes.
  • Boost Community Engagement: Your sponsorship fosters a vibrant, collaborative community.
  • Gain Visibility: As a sponsor, you gain recognition and enhance your reputation within the BLT and OWASP communities.
