About OWASP BLT

OWASP BLT (Bug Logging Tool) is an open-source platform dedicated to making the internet safer through community-driven bug reporting and collaboration. Users can report bugs, track issues, and collaborate with organizations to enhance security measures.

Earn points and rewards for your contributions. Organizations can launch bug bounty programs, reward contributors, and monitor trademarks. Our innovative token reward system, Bacon, recognizes valuable contributions.
